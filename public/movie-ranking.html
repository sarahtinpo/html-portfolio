The Best Movies According to Sarah

My top 3 movies of all time

Howl's Moving Castle

I love the animation and love story between Sophie and Howl. It has a few comedic elements which I enjoyed watching too.

The Painted Veil

Beautiful melodramatic movie about a couple who regains their love for each other in a tragic setting.

Miss Congeniality

Tomboy meets pageant contest. Hilarious move starring Sandra Bullock who's one of my favourite actresses. I can almost memorise the entire script.
